2. What Frontdesk Does

Frontdesk is an AI-powered call-handling service for HVAC businesses. When you miss a call to your Frontdesk number, Frontdesk sends an automated text-back, has an AI-driven conversation with the caller, attempts to capture job details, flags potential emergencies, and logs the call.

Frontdesk is not an emergency response service. It is not connected to 911 or any emergency dispatch system, and emergency detection is an automated, best-effort feature — not a guarantee.

If a caller describes a true emergency (fire, gas leak, carbon monoxide, or any life-threatening situation), you and your team remain fully responsible for responding appropriately and directing the caller to call 911. Do not rely on Frontdesk as your sole means of identifying or responding to emergencies.
