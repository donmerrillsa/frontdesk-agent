Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

1. Who We Are

Frontdesk ("Frontdesk," "we," "us") is an AI-powered call-handling service for HVAC businesses, operated by Don Merrill. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how you can reach us with questions.

2. Information We Collect

When you sign up for a Frontdesk trial or account, we collect:

  • Business name and owner name
  • Mobile number and backup contact number
  • Email address

When a customer calls your Frontdesk number, we collect and process:

  • The caller's phone number
  • Call details the caller provides (address, issue description, urgency, preferred time, system type)
  • Whether the call was flagged as a routine job or an emergency
  • The outcome of the call (booked, callback, no answer)

We don't collect this information for any purpose other than operating the Frontdesk service for you.

3. How We Use Information

  • To operate your trial or account, including provisioning your Frontdesk number
  • To run the AI call-handling and text-back service
  • To log calls and leads to your Google Sheet
  • To send you call alerts, trial reports, and account notifications
  • To send trial reminders and, if you choose to convert, billing communications

4. Text Messaging

By providing your mobile number, you consent to receive text messages from Frontdesk related to missed-call alerts, emergency alerts, trial status and reminders, and account or service notifications. Message frequency varies based on your call volume. Message and data rates may apply.

You can opt out at any time by replying STOP, or get help by replying HELP. We don't sell or share your phone number with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

5. How We Share Information

We share information with the following service providers, solely to operate Frontdesk:

  • Twilio — call routing and text messaging
  • Google — Sheet-based lead logging and reporting
  • Netlify — website and form hosting

We do not sell your information, or your customers' information, to third parties.

6. Data Retention

We retain trial and account data for as long as your account is active, and for a reasonable period afterward in case you reactivate. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Choices

You can opt out of text messages by replying STOP. You can request a copy of your data, or ask us to delete it, by emailing us below.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information, including relying on the security practices of Twilio, Google, and Netlify for the services they provide. No system is 100% secure, and we can't guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

Frontdesk is a business tool intended for HVAC business owners and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll update the "Last updated" date above when we do.
